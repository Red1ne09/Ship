Tips To Help You Speed Up Your ComputerBy @red_one_dev

Defragment Your Disk

This Makes Startup, App Opening and Game Loading Faster.

  • Press Windows + S & Write "defragment and Optimize Drives" and hit enter.
  • Select Your Disks To Defrag.
  • Press The "Optimise" Button To Defragment Your Disk (Takes Up to an hour).
  • Make Sure All Your Disks are 100% Defragmented.

Boost Power Usage

This Makes Hardware Consume More Power For Better Performance (FOR LAPTOPS)

  • Put & Keep Your Laptop In It's Charger.
  • Press The Battery Button From The Taskbar.
  • Turn Off "PowerSaving" Mode.
  • Move The Slider To "Best Performance".

Hardware Changes To Make

This Improves Everything Except Graphics.

  • 4GB in 2023+ is Useless, change it to atleast 8GBs.
  • If You Have HDD(Hard Disk Drive) change it To SSD (Incridible Speed Change).
  • Use Only One Browsering App Like "Google Chrome", "Opera GX", "MS Edje".
  • Make Sure All Your Disks are 100% Defragmented.
  • Renew CPU's thermal Paste and Clean The components (For Better Cooling)
